My Favorite Photos from 2016

Blaine Harper. American Dive. New Haven, Connecticut.

I spent two months in Connecticut this year. We would come down to this beach and swim
or fish almost every night.

Jake Wickersham. Wallride Nollie Out. Lawrence, Kansas.

Shooting other skatephotographers is always rad! Jake is an amazing young photographer
that is really starting to make things happen. He had several photos run in skate magazines
this year.

Zach Elder. Switch Bluntslide. Kansas City, Missouri.

I've been skating with Zach for about 15 years now. Zach is not only a great skater but also
an amazing videographer. He is currently working on a project for Chophouse Skateboards.
I can't wait to see it!

Jason Bontrager. Feeble Grind. Kansas City, Kansas.

We refer to this day as Feeblepalooza. We went to this rail to shoot a photo of my friend
Scotty smith grinding it. We didn't really expect Jason to feeble it that day. After Scotty got
his trick, Jason starting rolling up to the rail. About fifteen minutes later he rolled away perfectly.

Shawn Hale. Backside Noseblunt. Joplin, Missouri.

Shawn grew up in my hometown of Joplin, Missouri. Shawn is on the verge of turning pro
for Birdhouse Skateboards, Tony Hawk's company. The local skate shop flew Shawn in to
do a demo in August. It's amazing to see how much he has progressed.

Scotty Laird. Bluntslide. Irvine, California.

Our friend Yosef took Scotty and I to this ditch in Irvine. When he described it to us, I wasn't
really sure we should go there. I'm glad we decided to go. This was one of the raddest places
I've ever skated! Amazing!

Zale Bledsoe. Smith Grind, Kansas City, Missouri.

Zale is my best friend. This year we finally lived in the same town again and were able to skate
a lot. There's nothing I enjoy more than getting to take cool photos of my best friends!
